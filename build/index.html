AI-Powered PPTX Tool

Final Year Project – Under the Supervision of Ma'am Forzia

This project, AI-Powered PPTX Tool, is the culmination of our undergraduate journey. As a team of three—Usman, Manoor, and Faisal—we worked collaboratively and passionately to bring our vision to life.

Our mission was to develop a smart, user-friendly tool that simplifies the creation and enhancement of PowerPoint presentations using artificial intelligence. From ideation and planning to development and testing, we poured our energy and creativity into every stage of the process.

This project was completed under the valuable mentorship of Ma'am Forzia, whose guidance and encouragement played a vital role in our success. The experience has not only sharpened our technical and teamwork skills but also taught us the value of persistence, innovation, and collaboration.

We’re proud of what we’ve built and hope it makes the process of crafting impactful presentations easier and more accessible for everyone. Thank you for exploring our work!

How It Works

Learn how to create and enhance your presentations effortlessly

  • Step 1 — Create a Presentation

    Provide a prompt, and our AI-powered tool will generate a PowerPoint presentation tailored to your input. The generated presentation will be ready for download and use.

  • Step 2 — Enhance an Existing Presentation
